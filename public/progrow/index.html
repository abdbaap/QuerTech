Application Process

  1. Navigate to the Infosys Springboard website and locate the prominently displayed "Get Started" button, then click it to begin your journey.
  2. Initiate the registration process by accurately entering your date of birth, confirming that you meet the minimum age requirement of 10 years or older.
  3. Complete the personal details form, providing your full name, active email address, phone number, and establish a secure password for your account.
  4. Verify your email address by clicking on the unique verification link that will be promptly sent to your inbox, ensuring account security.
  5. Carefully review and explicitly accept the platform's Terms & Conditions and the Data Privacy Statement to proceed.
  6. Proceed to fill out a comprehensive registration form with essential demographic details including gender, state, city, educational institute, affiliation, current grade, and profession.
  7. Personalize your learning experience by selecting your areas of interest (e.g., Machine Learning, Cyber Security, Personality Development) to receive tailored course recommendations.
  8. Finalize your platform setup by customizing settings such as preferred language, font size, visual theme, and chatbot preferences to suit your individual needs.
