Monetag: Your Monetization Partner

Monetag is a cutting-edge advertising platform designed to help creators like you monetize various traffic sources. Whether it's from websites, social media, Telegram mini apps, or email campaigns, Monetag offers a robust solution to generate revenue from your digital content.

Direct Links: Your Path to Revenue

Monetag's "Direct Links" feature is a powerful tool. Create a unique link that leads directly to your monetized content. Share this link across social media, blogs, or anywhere you drive traffic, and start earning when users interact with the ads served.

Effortless Sign-Up Process

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Visit Monetag.com.
  2. Click "Start Earning" and sign up using your email or Google account.
  3. Complete your profile with personal or company details, including country and contact info.
  4. Specify your primary monetization method: Website, Social Traffic, or Telegram mini app.
  5. Provide relevant URLs or app names for verification.
  6. Accept the Terms and Conditions and pass the reCAPTCHA.
  7. Click "Sign Up" and get started!
