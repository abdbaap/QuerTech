Step-by-Step: Generating Your AI Videos

Follow this precise workflow to create your first AI-powered short-form video:

1. Crafting Your Narrative with ChatGPT

Begin by using ChatGPT to generate engaging story ideas. Use prompts like: "Write a short English story about an eagle" or "Create a 15-second vertical video script about overcoming challenges." For longer narratives, break them down into multiple, sequential prompts to maintain continuity. Always specify the desired aspect ratio (e.g., 9:16). Export your prompts as a `.txt` file for easy access.

2. AI Video Generation via Meta AI

Install and activate the "Auto Meta - Automation for Meta AI" Chrome extension. Navigate to the Meta AI interface. Utilize the "Select multiple images" option or the text-to-video prompt input. Paste your generated story prompts. This powerful tool can produce multiple video clips per prompt, automating the download process. Experience the magic of bulk video creation!

3. AI Voiceover with Google AI Studio

Head over to Google AI Studio and select the "Turn text into audio" feature. Choose the "Single-speaker audio" mode and select a voice that suits your tone (e.g., Zephyr, Leda). Paste your story text into the editor and generate the audio. Important Note: While AI video makers often have auto-sync features, they may not support custom AI voiceovers. Manual matching in CapCut is crucial for perfect lip-sync.

4. Seamless Editing in CapCut

Import all your AI-generated video clips and the voiceover audio file into CapCut. Drag the voiceover onto the timeline and then manually arrange your video clips in chronological order, synchronizing them with the narration. Enhance your videos with subtle effects (like "Sparkle") and smooth transitions between clips to boost visual appeal. Export your polished, engaging short-form video!
