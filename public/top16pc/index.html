2016's BEST PC Games Ranked!

Discover the definitive list of PC gaming's standout titles from 2016.

#10

Stardew Valley

PC, Switch, Mobile, Consoles

Escape the corporate grind for a new beginning in a charming small town. Explore, farm, mine, and build relationships in this beloved indie gem.

Strengths: Engaging gameplay, creativity, freedom.
Weaknesses: Can be slow-paced, basic combat.
Released: Feb 26, 2016
#9

XCOM 2

PC, PS4, Xbox One

Lead the resistance against alien occupation in this thrilling turn-based tactical combat and strategy game. Every decision matters.

Strengths: Deep tactics, strong narrative, tension.
Weaknesses: RNG frustration, steep learning curve.
Released: Feb 5, 2016
#8

INSIDE

PC, Xbox, PS4

A dark, atmospheric puzzle-platformer where you control a boy navigating a dystopian world filled with mystery and peril.

Strengths: Immersive atmosphere, narrative, clever puzzles.
Weaknesses: Short length, limited replayability.
Released: Jun 29, 2016
#7

Civilization VI

PC, Switch, Consoles

Guide your civilization from the Stone Age to the Information Age. Forge a legacy through war, diplomacy, science, and culture.

Strengths: Deep strategy, replayability, multiple paths.
Weaknesses: Steep learning curve, late-game pacing.
Released: Oct 21, 2016
#6

Titanfall 2

PC, PS4, Xbox One

Experience a unique blend of fast-paced first-person shooter action and giant mech combat. Master pilot and Titan synergy.

Strengths: Creative level design, fluid movement, pilot-Titan synergy.
Weaknesses: Smaller player base early on.
Released: Oct 28, 2016
#5

Dishonored 2

PC, PS4, Xbox One

Choose your path as Emily Kaldwin or Corvo Attano to reclaim your throne. Master unique powers and intricate level design.

Strengths: Multiple playstyles, unique powers, level design.
Weaknesses: PC optimization at launch, combat simplicity.
Released: Nov 11, 2016
#3

DOOM (2016)

PC, PS4, Xbox One

Rip and tear through demons in this reboot of the iconic shooter. Relentless, fast-paced, and brutal combat redefined.

Strengths: Fast combat, "Glory Kill" mechanic, soundtrack.
Weaknesses: Minimal story, inconsistent multiplayer.
Released: May 13, 2016
#2

Dark Souls III

PC, PS4, Xbox One

Embark on a challenging journey through a dark fantasy world to restore the Lords of Cinder. Master brutal, rewarding combat.

Strengths: Challenging combat, deep lore, atmosphere.
Weaknesses: High difficulty, indirect storytelling.
Released: Apr 12, 2016
#1

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - Blood and Wine

PC, PS4, Xbox One

An expansive expansion that adds a new region, a captivating storyline, and dozens of hours of rich content to an already legendary RPG.

Strengths: Beautiful world, engaging quests, rich storytelling.
Weaknesses: Extremely time-consuming.
Released: May 31, 2016
