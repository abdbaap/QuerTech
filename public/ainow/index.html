Your Actionable AI Roadmap

Don't just watch the future happen. Build it. Here’s how to start your AI adaptation journey:

Step 1

Prioritize AI Education

Dedicate time to understanding AI tools and their applications relevant to your industry or role. Aim for the '20-hour rule' for proficiency.

Step 2

Experiment & Integrate

Actively explore and integrate AI tools like ChatGPT into your daily tasks. Start small, but start now.

Step 3

Shift to Workflow Thinking

Re-evaluate job functions from a task-based perspective. Identify how AI can optimize processes and deliver outcomes.

Step 4

Embrace the Meta-Skill

Focus on learning how to prompt and interact with AI effectively. This meta-skill is increasingly valuable.
