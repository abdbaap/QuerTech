1 Daily Income Potential

2 Content Sourcing Strategy

  • Identify trending "community shorts" by examining popular YouTube Shorts channels (e.g., KickHitz, Gainz, SnipeFacts).
  • Crucially, find the original content creator by checking the video description for credits (e.g., "@Kyuvin_ On IG") on platforms like Instagram or TikTok.
  • Download these original, unedited clips directly from the credited sources.

3 Script Generation (AI-Powered)

  1. Copy the URL of the sourced YouTube Short.
  2. Use a "YouTube video transcript generator" website (e.g., youtubetotranscript.com) to extract the video's original spoken script.
  3. Paste the extracted transcript into an AI chatbot like ChatGPT, along with this specific prompt: 
    ChatGPT Prompt:"Rewrite this script in the same length with the same meaning but change the wording so it becomes 100% unique. Keep it engaging for YouTube Shorts commentary. Only give the rewritten script."
    This process ensures uniqueness for "fair use" purposes.

4 AI Voiceover Production

  1. Utilize ElevenLabs to generate an AI voiceover for the rewritten script.
  2. Select the "Alex" voice and adjust the speech speed (e.g., around 70-80% for clarity and engagement).
  3. Download the generated audio file.

5 Mobile Video Editing (CapCut)

  1. Import the downloaded original video clip into CapCut.
  2. Mute the original video's audio entirely.
  3. Add the AI-generated voiceover to the timeline.
  4. Incorporate visual enhancements:
    • Add graphic overlays like red circles or arrows (materials provided via WhatsApp channel) to highlight key visual elements.
    • Use "keyframes" to precisely track and move these overlays with the subjects in the video.
    • Apply "Fade Out" animations (e.g., 0.2 seconds duration) to overlays for smooth transitions.
  5. Integrate sound effects (e.g., "ding" for emphasis) at strategic points, synchronizing them with visual cues or commentary. Adjust their volume for balance.
  6. Add background music (also provided via WhatsApp channel), setting its volume to a lower level (e.g., 25) to ensure AI voice clarity.
  7. Implement auto-captions and adjust their style (e.g., size 12) and position for readability.
  8. Trim the video to its essential parts, ensuring the final output is concise and engaging.

6 Fair Use & Monetization

This method, focusing on original commentary and transformative editing of repurposed content, falls under "fair use" guidelines. This enables channel monetization without copyright strikes or re-used content issues.

Instead of creating content from scratch, identify popular short-form videos on platforms like Instagram and TikTok. Utilize the original creators' credits to access source material, transforming it with unique AI commentary and dynamic editing to fall within "fair use" guidelines.
