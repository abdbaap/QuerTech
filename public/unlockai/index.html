The CRISPE Method: Your AI Prompting Blueprint

C CONTEXT

  • Set the stage: provide business/situation info.
  • State the objective and desired background.
  • Specify the target audience for output.

R ROLE

  • Instruct AI to adopt a persona.
  • Example: "Act as a senior data analyst with 15 years of experience."

I INSTRUCTION

  • Break down the task into clear steps.
  • Specify desired output format (bullet points, paragraphs, etc.).
  • Define length requirements and tone of voice.

S SPECIFICATION

  • Define "how" the task should be performed.
  • Reiterate format, length, and tone.
  • Indicate specific elements to include.

P PERFORMANCE

  • Define metrics for success.
  • State desired outcomes or specific metrics.
  • Specify what to avoid.
  • Provide examples or benchmarks.
