Evaluation Methodology: How We Ranked Them

1. Context Awareness

Code generation is useless if the AI doesn't know your file structure. We prioritized tools like Windsurf and Aider that index your entire repository, not just the open file.

2. Agentic Capabilities

2026 is the year of the Agent. Tools that can run terminal commands, execute tests, and self-correct (like Roo Code) scored higher than simple "autocomplete" plugins.

3. Model Flexibility

The ability to swap models (BYOM) is crucial. Continue and Ollama win here by allowing you to plug in the latest open-weights models (DeepSeek, Llama) the day they release.

4. The "Free" Factor

We penalized tools with "Fake Free" tiers. Codeium scored highly for its "Free Forever" pledge, whereas Copilot's strict monthly caps lowered its ranking despite high quality.
